Data Analysis

            questionnaires completed by 20 Stay Here Hostel customers and the data obtained from the interviews with 2
            customers who had visited the hostel more than one time and who had stayed at the hostel more than one week.

            information about the service marketing mix (7Ps) used by Stay Here Hostel affecting the customers' decision
            to choose the hostel and the problems and recommendations from the customers, are presented as follows:

The Information about the Problems and Recommendations Concerning the Stay Here Hostel Service Obtained
                from the Questionaire Respondents.

            

                From the questionnaires, there were only 6 respondents from all the 20 respondents who gave comments on
                the problems they found. Those problems could be divided into 2 factors including the problems with the
                place and with the people. To begin, The respondents revealed that the breakfast which was provide with
                the room rent was not various. Some respondents stated that the room should be made as wide as Room
                number 2 and inside the room there should be provide curtains for every bed. One guest also complained
                about the water in the restroom. The guest complained that the shower in the restroom was cold and it
                took too long time to make it warm. The next problem was people factor. A respondent commented that some
                staff could not speak English; they could not give the information about the places nearby as well as it
                should be.
            

            

            

            
                
The Data Collected from the In-depth Interviews with a Customer who Had Stayed at the Hostel More
                    Than One Time and a Customer who Had Stayed at the Hostel More Than a Week 

            

            
The Data from the First Interviewee

            

                The first interviewee was Rikiya. He was a Japanese vlogger who had travelled many countries. This wass
                his first time in Chiang Mai and his first time staying at Stay Here Hostel. He had stayed at the hostel
                more than one week. He said that he found the hostel from the website named Booking.com.
            


            

                He was asked to specify the significant factors affecting his decision to choose Stay Here Hostel to be
                the accommodation for this trip. He said that he wanted a hostel which had a private room at a cheap
                price and was located in the old town area. He also was asked about the factors that might affect his
                decision based on the service marketing strategies (7Ps). He replied that he liked the decoration and
                the facilities of the hostel because the hostel provided working space downstairs; the price was
                reasonable. The location was also very nice because it was close to the public transport hub and tourist
                spot. However, he said that the promotion of the hostel was hardly found and he saw the hostel only on
                Booking.com which was the website collecting the hostels all over the world for backpackers. He also
                stated that the process of making a reservation and check-in and check-out were very easy. Staff were
                friendly, cozy and easy to talk to and they gave him all the information that he had to know. However,
                when the interviewer asked him what was the most important factor making him decide to stay at the
                hostel, his answer was the picture of the hostel in Booking.com. So, it can be concluded that the
                promotion of the hostel in terms of the advertising was the most influential factor affecting him to
                choose Stay Here Hostel.
            


            
The Data from the Second Interviewee

            

                The second interviewee was Zach, a marketer from United Kingdom. This was his second time staying at
                Stay Here Hostel. At first, he found the hostel from the website named HOSTEL WORLD which is a website
                collecting hostels around the world to serve backpackers. He first chose the hostel because he found it
                on HOSTEL WORLD and he found that the hostel was close to the airport and had many good reviews with
                beautiful pictures in the reviews of the guests who visited the hostel before. He also decided to come
                to stay at the hostel because of the staff. He said that the staff are very friendly. They always made
                sure that customers were feeling well.
            


            

                After the general questions, Zach was asked to reply the questions about the service marketing mix (7Ps)
                used by the hostel. With regard to the product or the hostel itself, he said that the hostel was very
                beautiful and, he liked the interior and the facilities of the hostel as well. In terms of the price, he
                said that the price was a bit cheap but what he got from here was really worth. He said about the
                location of the hostel that it was a bit hidden. When he needed a taxi to the hostel, many drivers
                refused to take him there. He also suggested that the hostel might have a problem about the location on
                Google Map.When asked about the hostel advertisement, he said that he never seen the advertisement of
                the hostel. He talked about the staff that they were very good and served him with hospitality. The most
                significant factor making him to stay at the hostel for the second time is the staff. He said that he
                can talk with everyone in the hostel. The staff always asked him “Where are you going?” , “ Where have
                you been?” that made him feel good.
