Rationale & Purpose

  Thailand has become tourist attraction for a long time especially Chiang Mai which welcomed millions tourists every year. The accommodation is a necessary part of a trip for travelers and hostels have become very popular among backpackers who want a low budget accommodation in a good location. Stay Here Hostel is a hostel in Chiang Mai which applied the service marketing mix (7Ps) welcomed many customers in 2019. However, there are many brand-new hostel in Chiang Mai. To be a leader of the market, the entrepreneurs have to create the strategy to influent the customers to choose their hostel. It might be better if the entrepreneurs know the customer's expectation and what influent them to select the accommodation. These results can lead to the interesting and effective strategy of the hostel. Therefore, this research aimed to study the effectiveness of the service marketing mix strategies used by Stay Here Hostel. To complete this research study, the following guiding questions were then generated.
